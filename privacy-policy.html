Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled within the "Finding God" mobile application (the "App").

1. No Personal Data Collection

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from its users. We do not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information. Your usage of the App is completely anonymous to us.

2. Third-Party Advertising (AdMob)

The App is supported by advertisements. We use Google's AdMob, a third-party ad network, to display these ads.

  • AdMob's Data Collection: AdMob may collect certain non-personal information to serve relevant ads. This data may include device identifiers, such as the Android advertising ID or iOS advertising identifier, as well as general usage information like the type of device and your IP address.
  • Purpose: This data is used by AdMob to provide personalized advertising.
  • Your Control: You can reset your device's advertising identifier or opt out of personalized advertising through your device's settings.
  • AdMob's Privacy Policy: For a more detailed explanation of how AdMob handles data, we encourage you to review Google's own Privacy Policy: https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/.

3. Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics services that track your personal usage. Your use of the App is not tied to any user-specific data.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
